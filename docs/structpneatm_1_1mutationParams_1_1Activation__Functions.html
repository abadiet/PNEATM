double pneatm::mutationParams::Activation_Functions::rate

The mutation ratio of activation functions.

The value of rate typically lies between 0.0 and 1.0, where 0.0 indicates that no activation functions will be mutated, and 1.0 means that every activation functions will be mutated in every possible instance.
